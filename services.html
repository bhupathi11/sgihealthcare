X-RAY

X-rays are a type of radiation that when directed at a person’s body creates pictures of the inside of their body. Your doctor may prescribe an X-ray for multiple health conditions – bone fractures, soft tissue abnormalities, problems to do with your lungs, or for a routine checkup.
